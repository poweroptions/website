About Power Options

Power Options is a private startup company based in Uganda dedicated to delivering innovative Renewable Energy solutions. The organization combines engineering expertise with modern digital technologies to improve productivity, sustainability, and operational efficiency for businesses and communities.

As part of its digital transformation strategy, Power Options established a dedicated software and mobile application development division known as ESANYALAZE, a name loosely translated as "Power".

ESANYALAZE - Mobile Development Division

Enterprise Applications

Designing scalable enterprise-grade Android applications that automate business processes and improve operational efficiency.

Business Systems

Developing custom internal applications that streamline workflows, data collection and organizational management.

Mobile Games

Creating engaging mobile games alongside consumer-focused Android applications for global audiences.

Innovation

Leveraging modern software engineering practices to deliver secure, reliable and high-quality mobile experiences.

Our Mission

To contribute to Uganda's sustainable development by combining renewable energy innovation with modern mobile technologies that solve real-world challenges.
